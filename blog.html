PRESSURE

What is pressure? It means different things to different people. To a science student, pressure is Force/Area OR density*acceleration due to gravity*height(or depth). Pressure is important in science. Without pressure, we won’t be able to pump water in most of our homes. Without pressure, you won’t be able to use straws to sip in drinks. Without pressure, you won’t be able to get injections (I’d very much like that, thank you).
